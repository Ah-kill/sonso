The logo should communicate:
- Speed (lightning, arrow, forward motion)
- Tech (clean geometry, circuit-like or grid-inspired)
- Custom/personal (feels like it was made for you, not mass-produced)
- The "So N So" name itself is distinctive — the logo should lean into the wordmark

**Logo concepts to explore:**
1. **Wordmark** — "SoNSo" in bold Inter with the middle "N" highlighted in green or treated differently (inverted, boxed, or with a motion line through it)
2. **Icon + Wordmark** — A lightning bolt ⚡ or a circuit node as the icon, paired with the wordmark
3. **Lettermark** — "SNS" monogram with geometric construction, in dark + green
4. **Abstract mark** — A single flowing node/connection shape that implies "process becoming system"

**Logo must work in:**
- Dark background (primary use)
- Light background (for documents/proposals)
- Single colour (for embossing, watermarks)
- Favicon/icon size (16x16, 32x32)

**Logo must NOT:**
- Look like a generic tech company (no blue globe, no pixel letters)
- Be too complex for small sizes
- Use gradients that break when printed

## 10. LEAD GENERATION PROCESS

### Stage 1 — Attract (Top of Funnel)

**Channels:**
- Facebook/Instagram paid ads (campaigns 1–4 above)
- Organic Instagram — reels, carousels, pain posts
- WhatsApp cold outreach to business owners in target industries
- Google Search ads — "custom software for [restaurant/salon/gym]"
- LinkedIn — thought leadership posts targeting MD/CEO/Owner titles

**Lead magnet:**
- Free 5-minute demo video of the most relevant product for their industry
- Free "How much is your SaaS costing you?" calculator (web tool)
- Free "Custom vs Off-the-shelf" PDF comparison

---

### Stage 2 — Capture (Lead Form)

**Facebook Lead Ad form fields:**
1. Name
2. WhatsApp number
3. Business type (dropdown: Restaurant / Salon / Gym / Retail / Other)
4. City
5. "What's your biggest operational challenge?" (open text, optional)

**Website CTA form fields:**
1. Name
2. WhatsApp number

**After form submission:**
- Instant auto-reply WhatsApp message within 60 seconds
- Message: "Hi [Name]! Thanks for reaching out to So N So 🙌 We'll get back to you within 2 hours. Meanwhile, here's a quick demo of what we build: [link]"

---

### Stage 3 — Qualify (Discovery)

**Qualification questions (asked over WhatsApp or call):**
1. How many people work in your business?
2. What are you using currently to manage [orders/bookings/inventory]?
3. What's the most frustrating part of that?
4. Have you looked at any software before?
5. What would "sorted" look like for you?

**Qualified lead criteria:**
- 10+ employees OR clear operational pain
- Decision-maker is the one we're talking to
- Budget awareness (they understand there's a build fee)
- Genuine operational workflow to solve

**Disqualify if:**
- Solo operator with no staff (too small)
- Just curious, no real pain
- Wants a job, not software

---

### Stage 4 — Convert (Proposal → Close)

**Steps:**
1. Send proposal within 24 hours of discovery call
2. Follow up on WhatsApp after 48 hours if no reply
3. Offer a 15-min screen share to walk through a relevant demo
4. Address pricing objection directly: "The build fee is one-time. Compare that to ₹15,000/month on Zoho."
5. Close with urgency: "We can start this week if you confirm by [date]."

**Objection responses:**

| Objection | Response |
|---|---|
| "It's too expensive" | "It's a one-time cost. Compare it to 12 months of Zoho — you'll be ahead in 3 months." |
| "We need to think about it" | "Totally fair. Want me to send you a cost comparison so you can think with numbers?" |
| "We already have Zoho/Shopify" | "Great — we actually work with a lot of businesses migrating off Zoho. What are you most frustrated with?" |
| "Will it work for our business?" | "That's exactly why we do a discovery call first. Tell me your workflow and I'll tell you exactly what we'd build." |
| "How long will it take?" | "7 days for a standard product. Custom builds are 2–3 weeks. We'll give you a firm timeline in the proposal." |

---

### Stage 5 — Retain & Referral

**30-day check-in:** WhatsApp message asking how things are going
**90-day check-in:** Ask if there's anything new they want to add
**Referral ask:** "If you know another business owner who'd benefit from this, we'd love an intro. We offer a referral bonus."

**Referral incentive:**
- ₹2,000–5,000 cash or credit for any referred client who signs
