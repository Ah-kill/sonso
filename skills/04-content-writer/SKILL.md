# So N So — Content Writer Skill

## What This Skill Does
Writes all social media content, captions, carousels, reels scripts, LinkedIn posts, and WhatsApp broadcast messages for So N So. Always aligned with the brand voice and the SONOSO_CONTEXT.md document.

## Trigger Phrases
- "Write a post about [topic]"
- "Create a caption for [platform]"
- "Write this week's content"
- "Draft a LinkedIn article"
- "Write a WhatsApp broadcast"
- "Create a carousel about [topic]"

---

## ALWAYS READ FIRST
Before writing anything, recall these from SONOSO_CONTEXT.md:
- Brand tone: Bold, human, fast, honest, anti-corporate
- Never use: synergise, leverage, cutting-edge, end-to-end, seamless
- Always use: short sentences, active voice, real numbers, pain-first structure

---

## Content Formats

### Instagram/Facebook Post (Caption)
```
[HOOK — 1 line, stops the scroll]

[PAIN or CONTEXT — 2-3 lines, they nod their head]

[BRIDGE — 1-2 lines, introduce So N So]

[PROOF or SPECIFICS — number, result, or example]

[CTA — one clear action]

#SoNSo #CustomSoftware #[IndustryHashtag] #SME #TechForBusiness
```

### Carousel (Slide-by-Slide)
- Slide 1: Bold hook (question or provocative statement)
- Slides 2–5: One point per slide, large headline + 2-line explanation
- Slide 6: The So N So solution
- Slide 7: CTA ("DM us" or "Link in bio")

### Reel Script (30–60 sec)
```
0–3s:   Hook text on screen + voiceover (pain or surprising stat)
3–15s:  Problem expanded — real scenario
15–25s: Solution shown (screen recording or visual)
25–35s: Key message ("7 days, one-time fee, zero maintenance")
35–45s: CTA + So N So logo
```

### LinkedIn Post
- Open with a counter-intuitive statement or question
- Tell a short story or use a real example
- Give a clear insight or lesson
- End with a soft CTA or question to drive comments
- No hashtag spam — max 3 relevant hashtags

### WhatsApp Broadcast
- Max 5 lines
- Personal tone ("Hi [Name]")
- One clear ask or share
- Never feel like bulk marketing

## Content Pillars (rotate weekly)

1. **Pain** — What your clients are suffering without good software
2. **Education** — How custom software works, what it costs, how fast it deploys
3. **Comparison** — So N So vs Zoho/Odoo/Shopify/agencies
4. **Social Proof** — Client results, case studies, testimonials
5. **Behind the Scenes** — How we build, what the team does, demos
6. **Industry Spotlight** — Deep dive into one industry per week

## Tone Examples

❌ Wrong: "Our cutting-edge solutions leverage AI to deliver seamless digital transformation."
✅ Right: "We built a dashboard for a 40-person logistics company. Their manager now checks 3 numbers every morning instead of calling 5 people."

❌ Wrong: "Optimise your workflow with our enterprise-grade platform."
✅ Right: "Still sending order updates on WhatsApp at midnight? There's a better way."
